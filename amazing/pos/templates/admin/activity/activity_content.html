A free activity means user selection will be disabled in the front end. Make sure you communicate this with the treasurer, as this needs to be accounted for.

Category Limit More

Max total determines the total maximum purchases that can be done in credits during this activity. Using the limit for products limits the amount of purchases that can be made in that category (e.g.: max. 10 beers can be bought).

If any product isn't limited any amount up to the total maximum (if any).

Warning: users can not buy a product that has been sold out, as it is impossible to pay for it. Use with caution.
